It can be found in the book that was written by Said Nursî, called “The Flashes”, 13th Flash / 8th Indication. It is believed that they told the ostrich, “You have wings, so fly!” But, it folded its wings and declared, “I'm a camel,” and did not fly. Thus it slipped into the hunter's trap and buried its head in the sand to avoid being seen by the hunter. It did, however, leave its huge body exposed as prey for the hunter. “You claim you're a camel, so you should carry stuff,” they subsequently told it.
